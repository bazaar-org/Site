Browse the way you want

Trying to find the perfect app? Bazaar has tons of ways to find what you want or what you didn't know you wanted. Scroll through Flathub, search through categories, or find similar apps through tags.

Find it fast

Search across all your Flatpak remotes from one place. Results show up instantly with the ability to install apps right there and then.

View it all

Screenshots, descriptions, download stats, and permissions. If app data is available, it is clearly presented on the app's page.

Your Library

Manage everything you've installed. Apply updates, uninstall apps, see queued downloads and find add-ons all in one page.
